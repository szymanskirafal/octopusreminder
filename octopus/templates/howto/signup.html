How to sign up.

Tap Safari icon. Write octopusreminder.com in the search bar and click Go button. The app should start. Then click the menu icon on top right corner.

Choose Sign Up option.

Type your email address, username and password. We won't sell your data and we won't spam you. Ever. Word.

Then you should see a statement that the app sent and email to you to confirm your signup.

Check your email. You should receive an email from the app within few minutes. Open it and click the link to confirm your signup.

New Safari window should open where you can confirm your email address.

Sign In page should appear with green statement that You have confirmed your email address. Username and password fields should be fullfilled with credentials you provided few steps before. We suggest to click Remember Me checkbox field to let the app remember you with no need to log in every time. Tap the blue Sign In button to finish the process.
