How to sign in.

Tap Safari icon. Write octopusreminder.com in the search bar and click Go button. The app should start. Then click the menu icon on top right corner.

Choose Sign In option.

The Sign In screen should appear.

You can type your username and password in appropriate fields.

It'd be good to click Remeber Me checkbox icon, so you won't have to sign in next time. The app will remember you. Tap Sign In blue button to finish the process.

Successfully signed as ... statement should appear and you can use the app.
