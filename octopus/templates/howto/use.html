How to use the app.

Tap the Octopus icon on your smartphone to start the app.

You can also type "octopusreminder.com" in your web browser on any device.

The app should start with "New thing" screen to let you write immidiately some important thing you want not to forget about.

If it's not then you should sign in or sign up. Maybe you try to use the app on the new device or you forgot to click checkbox icon "Remember Me" when you were login in last time. Here's the details how to sign in or sign up.

Type the important thing here.

The "Today" checkbox icon is set automaticaly in case the thing you write is super imprtant and should be done today. You can uncheck it if the thing can be done later.

Click Save and don't worry. The app will remember and it will try to remind you about them by sending you an email with list of things few times a day.

You will receive emails few times a day.

The email should look this one.

The list of important thing is divided into two parts. First part - Today.

Second part - Later.

Each thing has an "Edit" link. Click it to edit, change or delete the thing.

You don't have to wait for emails to see what things you should remeber about. Just open the app, click on the menu icon on top right corner and choose "List".

The list looks almost the same as the list you receive by emails. The only difference is the icon. Click it to edit, change or delete the thing.
