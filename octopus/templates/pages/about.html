The app helps to not forget about things. It does it in two steps:

1.

The app let you write notes fast, just after you start it. One click and it's ready. No settings here. There's no time to set up anything in this moment.

2.

Do you check your email regularly all the time? Imagine you have a friend who sends you an email every morning with list of things you don’t want to forget about. What a friend, right? OtopusReminder app sends you that kind of email few times a day: in the morning, while you’re at work and in the evening. You can change the schedule if you want.
